Forging Dutch-Chinese Business Alliances: Insights from SEAN and NCBC Event in Wassenaar

Posted by Xu Xiao Jia on 2022/03/19 19:00

On Friday March 22th, as the Chairwoman of the SEAN 'Stichting Europe-Asia Network (Foundation)' and the board member of NCBC 'Netherlands China Business Council', I participated in the event co-organised by CCPIT (China Council for the Promotion of International Trade) Guangzhou and NCBC in Wassenaar. Mr. Jin Yuan, Economic & Commercial Counsellor of China in the Netherlands, Mr. Boudewijn Poldermans 包德文, Vice chairman NCBC, Mr. Yang Yong, President of CCPIT Guangzhou and Mr. Hans Kuijpers, Director Investment Projects of NFIA gave speeches. During the event, the speakers and the guests discussed various topics: Deepen economic and trade cooperation with the Netherlands and EU countries; Promote Guangzhou Business Environment; Seek potential partners in the Netherlands; Enhance exchanges in business community... NCBC en CCPIT Guangzhou will continue to jointly promote Dutch-Chinese business activities in the Netherlands and China.
