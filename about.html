HollandChina Consulting helps Chinese and European companies/institutions find cooperation opportunities for development in both regions. We provide consulting services and establish business relationships between Europe (including the Netherlands) and China. In addition to providing relevant professional consulting services, we also offer special PR event services in China and Europe. We also provide relevant training services for enterprises and institutions. HollandChina Consulting helpt Chinese en Europese bedrijven/instellingen bij het vinden van samenwerkingsmogelijkheden voor ontwikkeling in beide regio's. We bieden adviesdiensten en leggen zakelijke relaties tussen Europa (inclusief Nederland) en China. Naast het verlenen van relevante professionele adviesdiensten, bieden wij ook speciale PR-evenementendiensten in China en Europa.
